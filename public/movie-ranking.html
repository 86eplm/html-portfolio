The Best Movies According to Eric

My top 3 favorite movies.

Ford vs Ferarri

The story of Caroll Shelby's efforts to build a car for Ford to compete against and beat Ferarri at the 1966 24 Hours of Le Mans race in France.

Rush

True story of famed Formula 1 driver, Niki Lauda, through his career in the 1970's and 1980's.

Chappie

A police robot is reprogrammed with the ability to think and feel, becomes captured and taught how to understand human behavior by gangsters.
